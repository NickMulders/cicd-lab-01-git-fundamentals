02
Life without CI/CD
Every one of these is real. You have probably lived at least three of them.

The backup strategy

backup_final_v3_REALLY_FINAL.gwbk

Manual gateway backups on a shared drive. The newest one wins. Probably.

The deploy pipeline

a USB stick and a prayer

Resources copied by hand, site by site, whenever someone happens to drive there.

The classic ticket

"It works on Site 3 but not on Site 7"

Nobody can diff two gateways, so nobody knows why. The ticket stays open for months.

The audit trail

"Who changed the alarm setpoint? When? Why?"

No history, no author, no rollback. The honest answer is a shrug.

The test environment

production

Every change is tested in production, by production. The operators find the bugs.

The bus factor

"Only Karl knows how that works."

Karl is on holiday. Nobody dares to touch anything Karl built. Karl built everything.

None of this is a people problem. It is a system problem. And systems can be fixed.
Let's name what Oatmakers is living with.
